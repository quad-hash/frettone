\# FretTone



FretTone is a small web app for checking guitar chord tones and scales on the fretboard.



ギター指板上で、コードトーンやスケールの構成音を確認できるシンプルなWebアプリです。



\## Demo



https://quad-hash.github.io/frettone/



\## Features



\* Display chord tones on a guitar fretboard

\* Display scales on a guitar fretboard

\* Choose root note

\* Choose chord or scale type

\* Switch between note names and degrees

\* Supports basic jazz chord types and scales



\## Supported Chords



\* M7

\* m7

\* 7

\* m7♭5

\* dim7



\## Supported Scales



\* Major

\* Natural Minor

\* Dorian

\* Mixolydian

\* Altered

\* Lydian Dominant

\* Half-Whole Diminished

\* Whole Tone



\## Why I made this



I wanted a quick way to see chord tones, scales, and degrees directly on the guitar fretboard while studying guitar and jazz theory.



ギターやジャズ理論を勉強するときに、コードトーン・スケール・度数を指板上ですぐ確認できるものが欲しかったので作りました。
